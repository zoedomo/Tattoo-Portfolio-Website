Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a tattoo?

Fill out the booking form on my website or message me through Instagram or Facebook to schedule your appointment!

How much do tattoos cost?

Pricing depends on the size, detail, and placement. Flash pieces are usually a set price, while customs are quoted individually after consultation.

Do I need to leave a deposit?

Yes, a non-refundable deposit is required to hold your appointment and before designs are started. The deposit goes toward the final price of your tattoo.

What should I do to prepare for my appointment?

Make sure you are well-rested, hydrated, and have eaten before your appointment. Wear comfortable clothing that makes the area you're getting tattooed easy to access. Please avoid shaving the area within 24 hours of your tattoo appointment to ensure the skin is in the best condition for the tattooing process.

Does getting a tattoo hurt?

Pain tolerance varies for everyone, but tattoos generally involve some discomfort. I always work with my clients to make the process as comfortable as possible.

How do I Pay?

I accept Cash, CashApp, or Venmo.

How do I contact you?

The best way to reach me during business hours is by calling the shop phone and asking for Zoe. If it’s after hours, feel free to message me on Facebook, Instagram, or email. I’ll get back to you as soon as I can!

Aftercare Instructions

Taking care of your new tattoo is crucial for proper healing and long-lasting results. Here's how to care for your ink:

  • Wash your hands before touching your tattoo.
  • Gently clean your tattoo with lukewarm water and mild, fragrance-free soap.
  • Pat dry with a clean paper towel — don't rub.
  • Let your tattoo breathe for a few minutes, then apply a thin layer of recommended aftercare ointment.
  • Wear loose, clean clothing over the area — avoid tight or abrasive fabrics.
  • Avoid direct sunlight, swimming, and soaking your tattoo until it's fully healed (about 2-4 weeks).
  • Do not pick, scratch, or peel the scabs or flakes!
  • Contact me if you have any questions or concerns during healing!

Follow these instructions carefully to ensure the best possible healing and a beautiful final result!
